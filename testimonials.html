John - September 2020
Two hrs from Glasgow and it’s a different world. Travel down past Dumfries and Dalbeattie and just watch the clock slow down. The entrance to the park is 50 yards past the turn off to Kippford village, don’t get confused with the other park. The lodge is located half way up the sloping site and all you need do is follow the road to the left until you reach the lodge. Parking for two cars. Spacious accommodation with a large open plan living area, three bedrooms and two shower rooms. Plenty of info available on local amenities and woodland walks. Views are stunning and it has the added advantage of a covered hot tub. Fantastic base for a very chilled out five day stay for me and my adult family, plus baby grand daughter. We had planned lots of touring while we were there. No need, woodland walks, strolls down to the village, lunches at the Anchor, cream teas at the wee golf club house. One step for a hint, there is access to a woodland walk at the top of the site, or pavement access to Kippford by walking straight through the mini roundabout below the lodge to pedestrian access across from the golf clubhouse, rather than making our mistake on day one of walking out of the front entrance. All in all, the host was very good with communication, the description of this spacious lodge is accurate and the location is perfect to get away from the hustle of the day to day. Thank you for a lovely stay.
