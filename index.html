Our Luxury Lodge is located near Kippford Village on the Solway Firth.

The village is just three miles from Dalbeattie in Scotland.

Kippford is an unusually narrow village on the shore of the estuary of the River Urr and is popular with yachtsmen. It is a charming waterside location and from Kippford you can take a stroll on the Jubilee footpath to the pretty village of Rockcliffe.

Local attractions include Coastal Walks, Golf Courses, and Bike Hire. All set in beautiful surroundings.
